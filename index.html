Introduction

Gothic A1 is a simple, yet elegant and versatile font by Korean type foundry HanYang Information & Communications. As a gothic font, Gothic A1 possesses certain properties. Gothic fonts tend to lack serifs and hairlines. Essentially, gothic fonts are formal fonts with no unnecessary decorations, and Gothic A1 is no different. As such, this font is useful in a variety of situations in which a simple and formal look is the most appropriate. This fontâ€™s versatility does not end here, however. As a Korean font, Gothic A1 also includes Korean Hangul characters. With that being said, it is a great font to use in situations where multiple languages are desired. Gothic A1 is the perfect font for anything that requires simplicity, elegance, and versatility.
